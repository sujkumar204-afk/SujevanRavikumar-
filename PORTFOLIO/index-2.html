03

Tamil Welfare Bank

Art Direction & Production Design · SketchUp & Blender

Inspired by Frank Capra's It's a Wonderful Life, this project reimagines the iconic Bailey Bros. Building & Loan as a Tamil cultural institution — the Tamil Welfare Bank. Built entirely in SketchUp and rendered in Blender, the environment is a fully realised interior space layered with cultural authenticity: terracotta-tiled floors, carved wooden pillars, Tamil scripture on the walls, and warm ambient lighting that evokes the intimacy of South Indian community spaces. Alongside the 3D renders, the project includes full technical production design documentation — scaled floor plans, section drawings, and detailed close-ups of architectural elements — demonstrating a complete art direction pipeline from concept through to build-ready specifications.
